## Usage

A word appears in the center; each new word enters from right to left with a soft blur and pushes the existing line until the full phrase locks centered. Drop it in as a chapter card, hero opener, or as a beat between scenes — the same headline + subtitle props power the whole text-animation family, so swapping motions takes one field change.
