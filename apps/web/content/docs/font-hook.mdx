## Usage

Font Hook swaps through a curated list of display fonts every few frames to
create a sharp, scroll-stopping opener. Use the `headline` field for your main
hook, and the `subtitle` field for a short tag line. The Style panel still
applies: the selected font is inserted into the rotation, and the background,
text, and accent colors drive the grid and accent bar.
