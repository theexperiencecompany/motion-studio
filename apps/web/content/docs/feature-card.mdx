## Usage

The atomic unit of any SaaS launch video. Stagger four of these in a SplitScene grid and you have an entire "Features" section.

Drop in an emoji as the icon (or any unicode character), give it a one-line title, and a single sentence of body. Match `accentColor` to your brand and `backgroundColor` to your scene background.
