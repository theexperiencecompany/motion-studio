## Usage

A demo slate that recreates the GAIA chat composer — a dark `bg-zinc-800` rounded-3xl input with attach (`PlusSignIcon`) and tools (`ToolsIcon`) buttons on the left and a circular send button (`ArrowUp02Icon`) on the right. The bar springs in, the prompt types out character-by-character with a blinking caret, the send button switches from the muted default to `accentColor` (default `#00bbff`) once text is present, then a mouse cursor enters from off-screen and clicks send. A pulse ring confirms the click.

Useful for product walkthroughs, onboarding videos, and "ask me anything" hero loops. Keep `query` short and concrete (≤ 70 characters reads cleanly without too much wrapping). `accentColor` controls the active send button color and the click pulse — pick a brand-aligned color.
