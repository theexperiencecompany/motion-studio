## Usage

The whole phrase glides in from the left as one compact move, while the words themselves are revealed in sequence only through opacity. Drop it in as a chapter card, hero opener, or as a beat between scenes — the same headline + subtitle props power the whole text-animation family, so swapping motions takes one field change.
